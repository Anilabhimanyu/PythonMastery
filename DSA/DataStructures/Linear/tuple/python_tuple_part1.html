4 ‚ñ∏ Modification & Updating

Tuples are immutable. To modify, convert to list and back:

t = (1,2,3)
lst = list(t)
lst[0] = 10
t = tuple(lst)  # (10,2,3)
5 ‚ñ∏ Deletion Methods

Cannot delete individual items. Can delete whole tuple:

t = (1,2,3)
del t
# print(t) -> NameError

9 ‚ñ∏ Tuple Comprehensions

Tuple comprehensions do not exist. Use generator expressions instead:

gen = (x*x for x in range(5))
t = tuple(gen)  # convert to tuple
print(t)        # (0,1,4,9,16)
